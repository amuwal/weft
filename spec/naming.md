# Naming — Linger

## Decision

**Name**: Linger
**Subtitle (App Store, 30 char max)**: "Stay close to your people" (25 char)
**Tagline (used in marketing / web)**: "A quiet place for the people who matter."

## Why "Linger"

1. **It's a verb.** Verb-named apps in this orbit work well (Calm, Stop, Tend, Reflect). Action-naming makes the value obvious: you *linger* over the small things you know about a person.
2. **Available in our exact category.** Only "Linger: Serious & Safe Dating" exists with this name — different category, will not be confused with us.
3. **Short, distinctive, easy to spell.** Six letters, two syllables, unambiguous.
4. **Sets emotional tone.** "Linger" suggests slowness, attention, fondness. Right register for a calm relationship-memory app.
5. **App Store SEO is handled by the subtitle and keyword field**, not the name. The subtitle "Stay close to your people" picks up *stay in touch* and *people*; the keyword field (100 chars, hidden) carries "personal CRM, friends, family, journal, reminders, contacts, memory, relationships, network."

## Rejected names and why

| Candidate | Reason rejected |
|---|---|
| Tend | 3+ apps already use it in this exact category (Tend: Relationship Companion / Garden / Connect). |
| Hearth | 4+ apps in adjacent categories incl. "Hearth: Ignite Deep Talks" — same lane. |
| Kindred | 5+ apps incl. "Kindred CRM" and "Kindred: The Private Circle" — direct competitors using this name. |
| Sonder | Two prominent apps already in connections/relationships space (Sonder Life, Sonder — find your people). |
| Foyer | Clear in the category but boring; doesn't evoke action. |
| Vellum | Strong brand, but multiple existing apps (wallpapers, AI assistant). Could cause confusion. |
| Plume | Plume Networks is a major WiFi brand — would cause search collisions. |
| Cairn | Cairn Hiking Safety is the dominant app with this name. |
| Closer | Multiple apps in dating/couples space — wrong lane. |
| Folio | Multiple wallet apps own the name. |
| Tether | Crypto brand. |
| Garden | Already a direct competitor in this exact category. |

## Trademark posture

Common-word verb in a different USPTO class from existing "Linger" marks. Recommend filing intent-to-use after TestFlight launch and revenue starts to look real (~3-6 months in). Lawyer up before $10K MRR.

## Other strong names worth keeping in back pocket

If during development "Linger" hits a blocker (trademark issue, App Store rejection, founder change of heart), the second-strongest available candidate from this research is:

**Foyer** — gathering space, entry to a home. Available in category. Less evocative than Linger but fine.

After that:
- **Plume** — soft, feathered. Brand collision with WiFi company is a real risk.
- An invented word — generate 20 of these only if all human-word options collapse.
