# Non-goals

The most important page in this folder. The way to keep v1 calm and beautiful is to be ruthless about what Linger is *not*.

## Linger is NOT…

### …a contact manager
Apple Contacts already exists. Linger doesn't store phone numbers, emails, or addresses by default. The unit is "memory of a person," not "rolodex entry."

### …a CRM
No pipelines, no stages, no "lead status." The market already has 50 of those. Avoid every word that exists in Salesforce.

### …a networking tool
No LinkedIn integration, no "people you might want to meet," no introductions feature. If you want to meet new people, use Bumble BFF or Sonder. Linger is for the people you already love.

### …a social network
Nothing is shared. No public profiles. No way to follow or be followed. The only person who sees your notes about Sarah is you.

### …a journal app
There's no "morning prompt," no mood tracker, no streak. Day One exists; if you want a journal of yourself, use that. Linger is a journal *about other people*.

### …a calendar
We don't replace calendar events. We may *integrate* with calendar (v1.2) to detect when you saw someone, but Linger doesn't schedule anything.

### …gamified
No watering plants. No streaks. No points. No achievements. No "level up." The metaphor "tend a garden" is rejected on purpose — it patronizes adult users.

### …an AI assistant
v1 has no AI. v2 might add Apple Intelligence on-device prompts. We will never:
- Send your data to a cloud LLM
- Auto-generate notes from your conversations
- Build an "AI relationship coach"

### …a couples app
Couples apps (Paired, Lovewick, Twig) are a different product. Linger is for *all* your important people, not your romantic partner specifically.

### …a family planner
Hearth Display, Cozi, etc. handle shared family logistics. Linger is private.

### …multi-tenant or team
No teams, no shared workspaces. One person, one device, one iCloud account.

## Things we will be asked for and will say no to

| Request | Response |
|---|---|
| "Can you add Slack integration?" | No. Linger is for personal life. |
| "Web app please?" | No. iOS-first is the brand. |
| "Bulk import from Notion/CSV?" | Eventually maybe; v1 no. |
| "Auto-message my friends for me?" | Absolutely not. |
| "AI write the message for me?" | No. The whole point is that you wrote it. |
| "Daily affirmation feature?" | No. |
| "Habit tracker tied to friendships?" | No. |
| "Make it Android?" | No (or not for a long time). |

## How to say no gracefully (when users ask)

Direct, warm, and brief. "Linger is intentionally small. We don't do that, and probably won't, because it would change what the app feels like." No apology, no roadmap promise, no debate.
