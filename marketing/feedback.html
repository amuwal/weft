Before you write

A short list of things people ask. If your question's here, you might not need to wait for a reply.

Can I get a refund? Apple handles subscription refunds. Tap your name in iOS Settings → Subscriptions → Weft → Cancel, then request a refund through reportaproblem.apple.com. If Apple says no and you feel it's unfair, write to us and we'll back you up.

Lost your notes? If you had iCloud sync enabled, sign in with the same Apple ID on a fresh install and the data will sync back. If you turned sync off and deleted the app, the local data is gone — and we genuinely cannot recover it. We never had it.
