Privacy, in plain English.

The short version: Weft writes to your phone. It doesn't write to us.

One paragraph summary.

What we collect

Nothing on our side.

The data you create lives in a SwiftData store.

iCloud sync (optional)

If you toggle iCloud sync on, Weft writes to your iCloud private database.

If you sign out of iCloud, Apple removes the local copy.

Apple's role

If you bought a subscription, Apple processes the payment.

The App Store also publishes aggregate, anonymized download counts.

What we don't do

  • No advertising — anywhere, ever.
  • No selling, renting, or licensing data.
  • No "anonymized analytics" sent to a third-party service.
  • No AI model is trained on your notes.
  • No public profile, no social graph, no leaderboard.

Your control

You can export every note to PDF or Markdown.

You can also tap "Delete all data" in Settings.

Children's privacy

Weft is rated 4+ in the App Store.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything, we'll update the date at the top.

Contact

Questions or worries? Write to hello@getweft.xyz.
