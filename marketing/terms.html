Terms, short and fair.

You bought an app from a small studio. Here's what that means.

One paragraph summary. Use Weft for its intended purpose — keeping personal notes about people in your life. Don't reverse-engineer, resell, or use it to harass anyone. Your subscription renews until you cancel. Refunds go through Apple. The app is provided as-is.

What you agree not to do

  • Don't redistribute, sublicense, or resell the app.
  • Don't reverse-engineer, decompile, or extract source code (except where Apple's standard developer terms or local law explicitly allow).

  • Don't try to break the paywall. If you find a bug, we'd rather you tell us and we'll buy you a coffee.

Subscriptions

Pricing. Free tier is free forever. Premium is $3.99/month or $24.99/year. The yearly plan includes a 7-day free trial; the monthly plan does not.

Renewal. Subscriptions auto-renew through your Apple ID until you cancel. Apple takes payment 24 hours before the period ends. You can cancel anytime in iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions, and the cancellation takes effect at the end of the current period.

Refunds. Refunds are handled by Apple under their refund policy. We can't issue them directly. If you feel you've been treated unfairly by an automatic charge, write to us — we'll help you file the Apple refund request.

Price changes. If we ever raise the price, your existing subscription stays at its current price until you cancel. New subscribers pay the new price. Apple notifies you of any change before charging.

Your data

You own everything you write into Weft. The notes, names, threads, and photos are yours. We don't claim a license to your content — see the Privacy page for the full picture.

You can export everything to PDF or Markdown with one tap and delete the app without warning. We won't email you to come back.

To the extent allowed by law, we're not liable for damages caused by using or being unable to use the app. If something serious goes wrong, please tell us — we'll do what's right by you.
